一帐一算 隐私政策

生效日期：2025年7月6日

本隐私政策适用于iOS应用"一帐一算"（以下简称"本应用"）。我们深知您的数据隐私至关重要。本应用旨在为您提供一个简洁、高效的记账与计算工具，在此过程中，我们严格遵循数据最小化原则，确保您的个人信息得到充分保护。

1. 应用信息

  • 应用名称：一帐一算
  • 开发者：简易流 (JianyiFlow)
  • 联系邮箱：jianyi8679@outlook.com
  • 应用类型：：效率工具/记账计算类应用

2. 数据收集与使用

2.1 我们收集的数据类型

  • 账目数据: 您在应用中输入的记账内容，包括金额、项目、备注等。这些数据仅存储在您的本地设备上。
  • 使用统计: 应用使用频率、功能使用情况等匿名统计数据，用于改进产品体验。这些数据不包含任何可识别您个人身份的信息。

所有数据收集均基于用户主动输入和自愿行为，并在用户明确知情的前提下进行。我们承诺不会收集任何与您个人身份直接关联的信息。

2.2 我们不收集的信息

本应用明确承诺不收集以下信息：

  • 个人身份信息（姓名、身份证号、护照号等）
  • 联系信息（电话号码、邮箱地址、通讯录等）
  • 财务敏感信息（银行账户、信用卡信息、支付密码等）
  • 生物识别数据（指纹、面部识别数据等）
  • 位置信息
  • 麦克风、相机权限数据（本应用不涉及这些功能）
  • 广告标识符或追踪数据
  • 社交媒体账户信息

3. 权限使用说明

当您导出账单为图片格式时，我们可能会请求访问您的照片库，以便将导出的图片保存到您的设备相册中。该权限仅在您主动导出时请求，且您可以选择不授予权限。在未授权的情况下，导出图片功能将不可用，但其他功能不受影响。

本应用通常不需要请求特殊权限即可运行。未来若增加新功能可能涉及权限请求，我们将会在应用内明确告知并征得您的同意。

4. 数据存储与安全

4.1 本地存储

您输入的所有账目数据和应用设置均默认安全地保存在您设备的本地应用沙盒内。这些数据完全由您控制，不会自动上传到任何服务器。卸载应用时，这些本地数据将被自动清除。

4.2 数据安全措施

  • 所有数据在本地设备上受到操作系统级加密保护。
  • 我们不进行数据传输，因此不存在数据传输中的风险。
  • 我们定期审查代码，确保应用安全，防止未经授权的访问。

5. 第三方服务

本应用不使用任何第三方分析服务、广告网络或数据收集工具。本应用的核心功能完全离线运行，不依赖任何第三方服务器。

6. 儿童隐私保护

本应用不设年龄限制，适用于所有年龄段用户。我们不会故意收集任何用户的个人信息。如果监护人对儿童使用本应用有任何疑问，请立即通过邮箱联系我们。我们建议监护人监督儿童的应用使用情况，并在必要时设置适当的限制。若我们意外获知儿童个人信息，我们将在确认后尽快删除相关数据。

7. 您的权利

您对自己的数据享有以下权利：

  • 访问权: 您可以随时在应用内查看您的所有账目数据。
  • 修改权: 您可以随时修改或更新您的账目数据。
  • 删除权: 您可以随时删除应用内的任何账目数据。
  • 导出权: 您可以将您的账目数据导出为可读格式（如CSV）。

8. 法律合规

本应用遵守相关的数据保护法律法规，包括但不限于：

  • GDPR（欧盟通用数据保护条例）：欧盟用户享有数据访问、修改、删除、可携带等权利。
  • CCPA（加州消费者隐私法案）：加州用户享有了解、删除和拒绝出售个人信息的权利。
  • 中国网络安全法：遵守中国大陆地区的数据保护法律法规。
  • Apple App Store审核指南：严格遵守Apple平台的隐私要求。

9. 政策更新

我们可能会不定期更新本隐私政策以反映我们服务的变化或法律要求的调整。重要变更将通过以下方式通知您：

  • 应用内弹窗通知
  • 应用更新说明
  • 官方网站公告

One Account One Calc Privacy Policy

Effective Date: July 6, 2025

This Privacy Policy applies to the iOS application "One Account One Calc" (hereinafter "the App"). We understand that your data privacy is of utmost importance. This App aims to provide you with a simple, efficient tool for accounting and calculation. In this process, we strictly adhere to the principle of data minimization, ensuring that your personal information is fully protected.

1. Application Information

  • Application Name: One Account One Calc
  • Developer: JianyiFlow
  • Contact Email: jianyi8679@outlook.com
  • Application Type: Productivity Tool / Accounting & Calculation App

2. Data Collection and Use

2.1 Types of Data We Collect

  • Accounting Data: The accounting entries you input into the app, including amounts, items, notes, etc. This data is stored only on your local device.
  • Usage Statistics: Anonymous statistical data such as app usage frequency and feature engagement, used to improve the product experience. This data does not contain any information that can identify you personally.

All data collection is based on user active input and voluntary actions, and is carried out with the user's explicit knowledge. We pledge not to collect any information directly associated with your personal identity.

2.2 Information We Do Not Collect

The App explicitly pledges not to collect the following information:

  • Personal Identifiable Information (name, ID numbers, passport numbers, etc.)
  • Contact Information (phone numbers, email addresses, address books, etc.)
  • Sensitive Financial Information (bank accounts, credit card information, payment passwords, etc.)
  • Biometric Data (fingerprints, facial recognition data, etc.)
  • Location Information
  • Microphone, Camera Permission Data (this app does not involve these functions)
  • Advertising Identifiers or Tracking Data
  • Social Media Account Information

3. Explanation of Permissions

When exporting your accounting data as an image, we may request access to your photo library in order to save the exported image to your device. This permission is only requested when you actively choose to export, and you may decline to grant access. If permission is not granted, the image export feature will be unavailable, but all other app functions will remain unaffected.

This App typically does not require special permissions to operate. If new features are added in the future that involve permission requests, we will clearly inform you within the app and obtain your consent.

4. Data Storage and Security

4.1 Local Storage

All accounting data and app settings you input are securely stored by default within your device's local application sandbox. This data is entirely controlled by you and is not automatically uploaded to any servers. When the app is uninstalled, this local data will be automatically cleared.

4.2 Data Security Measures

  • All data on the local device is protected by operating system-level encryption.
  • We do not transmit data, thus eliminating data transmission risks.
  • We regularly review our code to ensure app security and prevent unauthorized access.

5. Third-Party Services

This App does not use any third-party analytics services, advertising networks, or data collection tools. The core functions of this App operate entirely offline and do not rely on any third-party servers.

6. Children's Privacy

This App has no age restrictions and is suitable for users of all ages. We do not knowingly collect personal information from any user. If a guardian has concerns about a child's use of the App, please contact us immediately via email. We recommend that guardians supervise their children's use of applications and set appropriate restrictions when necessary. Should we inadvertently become aware that we have collected a child's personal information, we will take steps to delete that information as soon as possible after confirmation.

7. Your Rights

You have the following rights regarding your data:

  • Right to Access: You can view all your accounting data within the app at any time.
  • Right to Rectify: You can modify or update your accounting data at any time.
  • Right to Erase: You can delete any of your accounting data within the app at any time.
  • Right to Portability: You can export your accounting data in a readable format (e.g., CSV).

8. Legal Compliance

The App complies with relevant data protection laws and regulations, including but not limited to:

  • GDPR (General Data Protection Regulation): EU users have rights to access, rectify, erase, and port their data.
  • CCPA (California Consumer Privacy Act): California users have the right to know, delete, and opt-out of the sale of their personal information.
  • China's Cybersecurity Law: Compliance with data protection laws and regulations in mainland China.
  • Apple App Store Review Guidelines: Strict adherence to the privacy requirements of the Apple platform.

9. Policy Updates

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time to reflect changes in our services or legal requirements. You will be notified of significant changes through:

  • In-app pop-up notifications
  • App update descriptions
  • Announcements on our official website
